Who am I?


I'm a games programmer who loves making games just as much as I love playing them. I've been making games for the last 5 years, continuing to develop my skills as well as pick up new ones. I love to challenge myself and learn new things, so I'm always on the lookout for new topics that interest me and give me something new to learn and experience.
